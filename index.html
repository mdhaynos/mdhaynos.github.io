Our Favorite Choices

  • Can Bar Seattle

    A nautical-themed bar specializing in canned beer. They feature more
    than 50 types of canned beer and you can get a pretty killer sandwich
    to go with your drink. There's a captain's bell hanging on the wall and
    if anyone rings it, they must buy the entire bar a drink!

  • Capitol Coffee Works

    A great decor and a wonderful place to spend the afternoon.

  • Jack's Fish Spot

    Don't leave Seattle without tasting some seafood!
